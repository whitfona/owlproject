Streamline your calendar

Manage all of your events in one place. Keep track of client sessions, vacations, conferences, and anything else you need to schedule all in the same calendar. Access your schedule anywhere at anytime

All of Owl is mobile and tablet friendly, so you can view and update your schedule wherever you are. Send your sessions to your iCal, Google Calendar, or any other third party calendar using Owl’s secure, PHIPA compliant one way Calendar sync.

Complete your administrative tasks from your calendar

Create two-click invoices, generate receipts, and email them to your clients without leaving the Calendar. You can even write session notes from the Calendar.

Shared calendar for groups or admins

Multi clinician shared calendar for groups, customizable room booking view, and availability limits so administrative staff always schedules clinicians at the right time.

Online Booking

An option feature, Online Booking allows clients to book their own sessions into your calendar. Limit who, when, and which services they can book so you remain in control of your schedule.

Automatic invoices and professional receipts

One click invoicing and third party billing company friendly invoices and receipts.

Integrated payment processing

Balance owing management
