Core Features

Customer Support

We’re here to help seven days a week. Customer support is included in your monthly fees so contact us today.

PHIPA and College Compliant

Store your client information on our PHIPA and college compliant servers.

Workflow Automation

Never miss a task again with your personalized task list. Track attendance, unpaid sessions, unsigned notes, and more.

Dashboard with Practice Financial Data

A healthy practice is more than the numbers, but we’ll help manage yours. See key stats graphed and export your data at any time.

Office Admin Access

Free access for office admins so you can be supported with scheduling and billing. Hide your session notes from your admins if needed.

Multiple Supervisor Access

Account for your supervisor on your sessions, financial documents, and session notes, and set your supervisor up with access to your Owl account to review and sign notes.
