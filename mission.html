Our Story

Since day one, Owl has been focused on helping therapists succeed in private practice. Owl was founded in Toronto by Andrew Sloss in 2012.

Andrew helps manage his wife Christine’s growing psychology practice. After years of managing client records on paper and using a variety of scheduling, billing, and note tools, they began looking for an integrated solution with all of their tools in one place. Most importantly, they needed a solution that was PHIPA and college compliant.

Instead of adapting several tools, they built their own! Since then, Owl has grown to support and empower thousands of Canadian mental healthcare private practices

Who we are

Behind Owl is a team of psychologists, therapists, engineers, designers, technologists and business managers who believe there is a better way to be in private practice. Everything we’ve learned about how to run a successful practice is built into the core of Owl so any clinician anywhere can leverage Owl’s world-class platform. And as a software-as-a-service offering, Owl keeps getting better every month. And it’s all designed, built and supported in Canada.
